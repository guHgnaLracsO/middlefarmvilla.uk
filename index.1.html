About

Middle Farm Villa B&B, is a small friendly family run bed and breakfast hosted by Jane and John. It is an 18th century barn conversion which has achieved four stars for its high quality beautifully decorated rooms. Both rooms are ensuite with a fresh contemporary styling and all the necessities expected of today’s traveller. Perfect for business or pleasure.

Chelveston is located in a lovely area of Northamptonshire, offering plenty of countryside walking and points of historical interest to visitors. Positioned on the village green directly opposite the pub, which is great for pub food or a quick drink, the Star and Garter has a large beer garden and an extensive food menu.

So if you need to catch up with family or friends, go wild in the country or need a comfortable place to stay while travelling for work, you can expect warm hospitality and a hearty breakfast to boot. Breakfasts are freshly cooked to order with the finest ingredients.
